# /goal 7 — Commercial Release Readiness

Prepare a production candidate, not an MVP demo.

Deliver:

- privacy/support/security policies aligned with the binary
- complete MASVS mapping and external review closure
- signed SBOM/provenance/release manifest
- full migration/recovery/device/soak reports
- staged rollout and rollback runbook
- completed docs/22-go-live-checklist.md with evidence links

Do not mark complete with open critical/high security findings, P0/P1 defects, missing device evidence, or unreviewed safety/privacy declarations.
